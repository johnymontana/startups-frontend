Startup Program

The next generation of world-changing ideas will be powered by graphs - and we're here to give yours a boost through free and discounted access to the World's Leading Graph Database.

Thanks!

Thanks for submitting your application to the Neo4j Startup Program. We'll process your application within the next few days and respond via email.

Questions?

Reach out to us at startups@neo4j.com if you have any questions about your application or status. We are not able to provide technical support at this address. If you have technical questions, please post them on community.neo4j.com

Who's eligible?

Whether you're just getting started or you have a couple million in revenue, Neo4j wants to help power both customer-facing and internal applications with graphs!

Requirements:

  • 50 or fewer employees
  • Less than $3MM USD annual revenue

What does Neo4j provide?

Startups in the program can build their applications on Neo4j Enterprise Edition, enabling mission-critical applications in a clustered environment.

Included for Free:

  • Neo4j Enterprise for 3 machines in production, 3 in test/staging and 6 in dev
  • Each machine can have up to 256GB of RAM and 24 cores
